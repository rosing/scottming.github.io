昨天正式开始我的Python之旅，先学了点命令行入门，说实话，在mac下写代码真是太美了，非常喜欢这种简洁的哲学。

命令行入门代码：pwd、ls、cd、mkdir、cd..

	Macbook% pwd
	/Users/Scott
	Macbook% cd temp
	Macbook% pwd
	/Users/Scott/temp
	Macbook% ls
	exl.py	stuff
	Macbook% cd ..
	Macbook% pwd
	/Users/Scott
	Macbook% cd ..
	Macbook% pwd
	/Users
	Macbook% cd ..
	Macbook% pwd
	/
	Macbook% cd ~
	Macbook% pwd
	/Users/Scott
	Macbook% 
	
写的第一个小程序：

	print "Hello World!"
	print "Hello Again"
	print "I like typing this."
	print "This is fun."
	print 'Yay! printing.'
	print "I'd much rather you 'not'."
	print 'I "sad" do not touch this.'
	
跑起来：

	Macbook% python exl.py
	Hello World!
	Hello Again
	I like typing this.
	This is fun.
	Yay! printing.
	I'd much rather you 'not'.
	I "sad" do not touch this.
